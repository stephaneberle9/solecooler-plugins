# The Offer Shell

The Offer Structure that monetizes access, not stuff.

## Why Shell vs Suite

**Suite** = Multiple separate products (course, mastermind, program, community)
- Improvements in one don't bleed into others
- Demands divided attention
- Multitasking = less happiness

**Shell** = ONE offer with different levels of access
- Improvements bleed into every level
- Stay in flow longer
- Focus on your art

## What the Shell Gives You

1. **Keeps you in flow** - So you're happier
2. **Sells access to YOU** - The most desirable thing you have
3. **Rewards focus** - Less work, more money

## The Irresistible Offer

Imagine dropping all boundaries today and selling unlimited access to you for a flat fee. Calls. DMs. Lunch every day. 5 spots only.

The key isn't finding a good offer—you already have one. The key is selling it without crossing your personal boundaries.

## Tiers

Pick what serves you at your stage:

| Level | What It Is | Example Pricing |
|-------|------------|-----------------|
| One-Time | Workshop/Asset only | $100-500 |
| Temporary | Limited time access | $500-3000 |
| Ongoing | Continuous access | $100-400/week |
| Premium | 1:1 access | 2x your group rate |

**Note:** Everyone gets the same STUFF. The difference is how much ACCESS they get on top of it.

## Front End (Getting Buyers)

### The Problem: Offer Fatigue
When people know what you'll say, they stop listening. Never promote an offer more than 2 weeks in a row.

### The Solution: Cycle Offers
Cycle through levels of your Shell:

**6 offers to rotate:**
1. **Core Offer** - Your Google Doc Offer
2. **Workshop** - Training you'll do for clients anyway
3. **Waitlist** - If Core Offer sells too well or too bad
4. **Level of Access** - Vary when others feel exhausted
5. **Recycle** - Successful workshops/courses (run them multiple times)
6. **Bundle** - Workshops/modules that work together

### Duration Rules
- No minimum duration
- Usually 1 week each between Client Offer and Customer Offer
- Maximum 3 weeks (except special events like Black Friday)
- Build cadence around YOUR life

### Life-Based Offers (Protagonist Style)
- Birthday Weekend Offer
- The Summer $10k
- OMAR (One Month All Recurring)

## Back End (Upselling)

### The Money Line

```
Workshop → Temporary Access → Ongoing → Premium/1:1
```

**Key stats:**
- 80% of clients come from a workshop
- 25% of workshop buyers become clients
- 15% of clients buy something else
- 5% of clients become 1:1 clients

The further end of The Money Line = more money, LESS selling.

### Upsell Methods

**Credit Forward:**
> "You can credit the payment you've made (and cancel the next $500 one in 6 days) to [Higher Tier]. So you save $1000 and your rate goes down to $X/week."

**Workshop/Asset Funnel:**
Store in community classroom, leads to next tier.

**Long Term Deals:**
Offer discounted rates for longer commitments.

**1:1 Coaching:**
For clients who want maximum access.

### Upselling Principles

- Don't try to upsell everyone (unrealistic)
- Goal: Extract maximum from everyone WILLING to pay
- The number is unknown to you AND them
- Too much selling to current clients damages relationship

**Example Win:**
Someone bought a $3k Temporary Access offer. Did not upgrade. Under Suite = loss. Under Shell = WIN (extracted $3k from someone not ideal for higher tier).

## The 2-Step Plan

Simple rules to find each person's maximum:

1. **Make a Front End offer every day**
2. **Make a Back End offer every week**

These give clarity and ensure you're doing the main thing: MAKING OFFERS.
