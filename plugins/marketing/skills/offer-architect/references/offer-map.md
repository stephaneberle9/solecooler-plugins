# The Offer Map (Google Doc 314)

The 4-part framework for crafting compelling offer copy.

Good copy isn't written. It's assembled.

---

## 1. PROMISE

The #1 thing to get right. One promise only—two halves effectiveness.

### The Meaning Test

Show your promise to two people. Does it mean the same thing to both?

| Fails Test | Passes Test |
|------------|-------------|
| Transform your physique | See your six pack |
| Achieve your fitness goals | Lose 15 pounds |
| Create your Personal Monopoly | Make $10,000 |
| Become a master of productivity | Finish your workday by noon |
| Find your ideal partner | 10 Dates in 10 Weeks |

### The "So You Can" Bridge

If your offer isn't tangible, use the 3 most powerful words in copywriting:

- Get rid of your worst habit... **so you can** lose 15 pounds
- Install the 3 AI Workflows... **so you can** make more than last quarter
- Let us implement a VA... **so you can** stop sending cold DMs

### Choose Your Frame

| Frame | Method | Example |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Coaching | "I'll help you find the answer within you" | "Our goal is to help you save 20 hours per week to spend more time with the people and things you love" |
| Consulting | "This IS the answer" | "We aim to build your $1M/year business with 95% margins, 67 clients or less, no more than 4 hours/day" |
| Mentorship | "This is what I'D answer" | "I'll be your Chief Financial Copilot so you make better decisions, faster" |

### The Mundanity Rule

**Content = ELEVATED** (time freedom, flow, happiness, being present)
**Offer = MUNDANE** (specific outcomes + numbers)

They need to tell their spouse something justifiable:
- "I hired a coach to be done by noon" (mundane, justifiable)
- "I hired a coach to become a productivity master" (elevated, weird)

| Elevated Content | Mundane Offer |
|-----------------|---------------|
| Time and location freedom | $5k in 5 Weeks |
| Be present with family | $84k/year |
| Flow state in your work | $20k one-time + $10k recurring |

### Mundane = Outcome OR Number

| Niche | Outcome | Number |
|-------|---------|--------|
| Productivity | Finish workday by Noon | Reduce to 2 hours max |
| Mindset | Get rid of 1 limiting belief so you can make more | Get rid of 1 limiting belief so you can double business |
| Health | Remove acne | Get testosterone to 1000 µg |

### Promise Hierarchy

1. **Small promise** > Vague promise (small is easy to keep; "I sold more with $20k promise than $30k")
2. **Aspirational promise** > Vague promise (people crave direction more than outcome)
3. **Generic promise** > Vague promise (swipe one if stuck)

## 2. PATH

Show them HOW you'll get them to the promise.

### Benefits, Not Features

| What They DO | What They GET |
|--------------|---------------|
| Office Hours and Community | Ask any question, get solution within 24 hours |
| Hours of trainings | Tools that allowed me to lose 100 lbs and keep it off 6 years |
| Complete clarity and manuals | An AI Chatbot adding $10k profit in <1 hour/week |
| 30 Approaches | 4 Dates in 4 Weeks |

### Structure: 3 Stages, 3 Parts Each

Each stage needs:
1. **Benefits** (what they GET)
2. **Checkpoint** (straight line to Promise)
3. **Timeframe** (when they'll hit it)

### Checkpoints Must Connect

Bad example (disconnected):
```
Phase 1: Belief work
Phase 2: Strategy sessions
Phase 3: Implementation
```

Good example (straight line to "Lose 30 lbs"):
```
Phase 1: Lose first 10 lbs
Phase 2: Lose next 10 lbs
Phase 3: Lose final 10 lbs
```

### Timeframe

Two numbers:
1. **Minimum** for first win (keeps people, reduces churn) - e.g., 16 weeks
2. **Full transformation** time (North Star) - e.g., 52 weeks

## 3. PRICING

Weekly beats monthly. Evidence exists. Reverse doesn't.

| Scenario | Weekly Price |
|----------|--------------|
| Doing 1:1, want to do group | Half your 1:1 price |
| Doing group, want to do 1:1 | Double your group price |
| Taking it slow early | $100-200/week |
| Can command higher prices | $250-400/week |

### When to Raise

Find your Freedom Number, raise every 25% checkpoint.

---

## 4. WHY NOW

Without urgency, people won't move.

### Scarcity
- Cap spots every intake
- Cap spots globally

### Urgency
- Open/close monthly (or randomly)
- Deadline for every intake
- Raise prices periodically

### Combined Tactics
- Limited spots (evergreen or current)
- 1-on-1 spots available
- Price raise at date
- Periodic price raises
- Closing doors at date
- Seasonal urgency
- Never available again
- Bonuses (add or take away)

## Offer Map Checklist

### Promise
- [ ] Passes The Meaning Test?
- [ ] Chose frame: Coaching, Consulting, or Mentorship?
- [ ] Mundane with Outcome or Number?

### Path
- [ ] Benefits (what they GET, not DO)?
- [ ] Checkpoints (straight line to Promise)?
- [ ] Timeframe (first win + full transformation)?

### Pricing
- [ ] Settled on weekly rate?
- [ ] Know when you'll raise?

### Why Now
- [ ] Clear scarcity?
- [ ] Clear urgency (deadline)?
- [ ] Price raise scheduled?
