## Email 3: Teach

**Send:** Day 4
**Subject:** The content pillar mistake that kills your reach
**Preview:** Most people pick 5-7 pillars. That's too many.

Here's what I see people do wrong with content pillars all the time:

They pick too many.

"I'll post about marketing, mindset, productivity, leadership, personal stories, industry news, and hot takes."

That's 7 topics. Your audience has no idea what you're about. The algorithm has no idea what you're about. You have no idea what you're about.

The fix is simple. Pick 3 pillars. Maximum.

Here's how to choose them:

1. What do you know deeply? (expertise)
2. What does your buyer care about? (relevance)
3. Where do those two overlap? (your 3 pillars live here)

Go back to the template. If you have more than 3 pillars filled in, cut until you're at 3. It feels limiting. It's actually freeing — you'll never stare at a blank screen wondering what to post again.
