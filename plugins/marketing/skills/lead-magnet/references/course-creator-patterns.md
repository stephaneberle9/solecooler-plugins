# Course Creator and Coaching Lead Magnet Playbook

What the top information product creators do to turn strangers into subscribers — and subscribers into buyers.

## Russell Brunson (ClickFunnels)

### The Free-Plus-Shipping Book

Brunson popularized offering physical books (DotCom Secrets, Expert Secrets, Traffic Secrets) where the reader pays only shipping costs.

| Why it pulls | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Physical object | A real book in hand feels far more valuable than another PDF |
| Micro-commitment | Paying even a few dollars for shipping crosses a psychological threshold |
| Extended engagement | Books take hours to read, building a deep relationship over time |
| Upsell architecture | The order form offers bumps and one-click upgrades |

**How it connects to the paid offer:** Each book teaches the methodology — ClickFunnels is the tool that implements it.

### The Challenge Model

The "One Funnel Away Challenge" runs 30 days with daily training from Brunson and his team.

| Why it pulls | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Action-driven transformation | Doing the work produces real results |
| Daily contact | 30 consecutive touchpoints cements the relationship |
| Peer accountability | Group participation keeps people showing up |
| Coaching preview | Participants experience the teaching style firsthand |

**How it connects to the paid offer:** The challenge builds their first funnel — they need ClickFunnels to build more.

### Brunson's Core Principles

| Principle | What it means |
|-----------|--------------|
| Value Ladder | The lead magnet sits on the bottom rung; each subsequent step asks for more commitment |
| Hook, Story, Offer | Every lead magnet needs a compelling hook, a relatable story, and a clear next step |
| Attractive Character | The lead magnet should introduce your personality and worldview |

## Amy Porterfield (Online Marketing Expert)

### The Actionable Checklist

Her "Must-Have Lead Magnet Launch Checklist" is brilliantly meta — a lead magnet that teaches you to create lead magnets.

| Why it pulls | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Do-it-now format | Checklists demand immediate action |
| Tight scope | Targets launching specifically, not marketing in general |
| Complete solution | Solves the checklist problem fully, not partially |
| Low consumption effort | Skim it, use it, done |

**How it connects to the paid offer:** The checklist helps create one lead magnet — her course teaches the entire online business system.

### The Live Training Approach

Porterfield runs webinars as her primary lead magnet, converting viewers into course students.

| Why it pulls | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Proof of teaching skill | 60-90 minutes demonstrates whether she can actually teach |
| Event energy | Live delivery creates urgency and engagement |
| Time investment | Extended viewing builds significant relationship capital |
| Natural pitch window | The final segment transitions smoothly to the course offer |

### Porterfield's Core Principles

| Principle | What it means |
|-----------|--------------|
| Specificity over breadth | Narrow promises outperform comprehensive ones |
| Prove it first | She demonstrates every concept before teaching it |
| Start where they are | Meet the audience at their current level, not where you wish they were |

## Ryan Levesque (ASK Method)

### The Segmentation Quiz

Levesque built his entire business model around using quizzes as the primary lead magnet. His "bucket" approach sorts prospects into distinct categories.

| Why it pulls | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Personalized experience | Results feel built specifically for that person |
| Audience intelligence | Every answer teaches you something about your market |
| Self-discovery drive | The desire to learn about ourselves is nearly universal |
| Targeted follow-up | Segmentation data transforms generic emails into relevant ones |

**The mechanism in four steps:**
1. Present diagnostic questions that reveal the prospect's situation
2. Sort answers into 3-5 distinct categories
3. Deliver customized results with specific recommendations for each category
4. Follow up with messaging tailored to their category

**How it connects to the paid offer:** The quiz pinpoints their problem — the product solves that exact problem.

### Levesque's Core Principles

| Principle | What it means |
|-----------|--------------|
| Ask before telling | Diagnosis comes before prescription — always |
| Small steps build momentum | Micro-commitments increase engagement at every stage |
| Right message, right person | Segmentation makes every communication feel personal |

## Recurring Patterns Across Course Creators

### What formats dominate

1. **Quizzes** — High engagement, enable segmentation for targeted messaging
2. **Multi-day challenges** — Create tangible transformation, build community
3. **Video training** — Demonstrate teaching ability, build personal connection
4. **PDF frameworks** — Quick wins, fastest to produce
5. **Free-plus-shipping books** — Maximum perceived value, micro-commitment entry point

### What these creators share in common

- Tight scope wins over broad coverage every time
- Deliver a genuine result fast, not a teaser
- Introduce your personality early — the "attractive character" matters
- Give a taste of the transformation the full program provides

### The universal connection pattern

Every info product lead magnet follows the same bridge:

**Lead magnet delivers a small transformation — Paid product delivers the full transformation**

The lead magnet demonstrates that the methodology actually produces results. The paid product is simply "more of what already worked."

## Building These by Difficulty Level

**Fastest to launch:** PDF checklists, frameworks, curated collections

**Highest conversion potential:** Quizzes, multi-day challenges, live coaching sessions

**Strongest for relationship building:** Video series, challenges, complimentary coaching

### Technology needed by format

| Format | What you need |
|--------|--------------|
| PDF downloads | Design tool (Canva), email platform |
| Interactive quizzes | Quiz builder (Typeform, ScoreApp, Bucket.io) |
| Multi-day challenges | Email platform, possibly a community space |
| Video training | Video hosting, email platform |
| Live webinars | Webinar software, email platform |
